About Me: Profiles and Journey

Hi! My name is Pranav Raj, a software developer with a strong engineering foundation from IIT Patna. I specialize in designing scalable and efficient web and backend systems, leveraging modern technologies like React, Go and Python to build robust solutions.

At Zeotap, I worked on optimizing APIs by converting them from synchronous to asynchronous, reducing latency by over 50%. I also developed a containerized monitoring stack with custom SQL queries, cutting issue detection time by 95% with automated alerts on Slack and PagerDuty. During my internship at Coinbase, I created an interactive dependency graph tool adopted by 15+ teams, which improved data retrieval efficiency by over 50%.

In addition to professional experience, I’ve worked on impactful projects. I engineered a Kanban board for QuickSell, enabling real-time grouping and sorting, while boosting rendering speeds by 40% with optimized React architecture. I also built an AI-powered article summarizer that achieved over 90% accuracy, integrating history tracking and error handling to enhance usability.

In competitive programming, I have achieved notable milestones: Expert status on Codeforces, a 5-star rating on CodeChef, and the Guardian title on LeetCode with a peak rating of 2426. My team ranked 115th in ICPC 2024 Prelims, and I secured global rankings of 36 in TCS CodeVita and 404 in the Meta Hacker Cup.

Beyond technical work, I excel in collaboration, having led cross-functional teams and contributed to projects requiring precision and teamwork. My leadership roles at IIT Patna have honed my ability to manage and deliver impactful projects.

Committed to continuous learning, I stay updated with industry trends to create innovative, scalable, and maintainable software solutions.
