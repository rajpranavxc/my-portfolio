About Me: Profiles and Journey

Hi! My name is Pranav Raj. As a seasoned software developer with a strong foundation in Engineering from IIT Patna, I specialize in designing and building high-quality web and mobile applications. My skill set includes modern technologies like React, Go, and Python, which I use to create scalable, efficient, and user-friendly solutions.

During my internship at Coinbase, I successfully developed and implemented an interactive dependency graph tool that was adopted by multiple teams, significantly enhancing development efficiency. I am proficient in both frontend and backend development and have hands-on experience with cloud-based solutions like Amazon S3, enabling me to build robust and secure applications.

I am also actively engaged in competitive programming, earning accolades on platforms such as Codeforces, CodeChef, and LeetCode. This experience has sharpened my problem-solving abilities and deepened my understanding of data structures and algorithms.

Collaboration and teamwork are central to my approach, and I have consistently demonstrated the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams to ensure timely and successful project delivery. My leadership roles at IIT Patna further highlight my capability to manage and lead projects efficiently.

I am committed to continuous learning and staying abreast of industry trends. This commitment drives me to explore new technologies and refine my development processes, ensuring that the software solutions I create are innovative, maintainable, and scalable.

In summary, I am a highly motivated software developer with a strong blend of technical expertise and soft skills, ready to tackle complex challenges and deliver high-quality solutions that meet the needs of clients and end-users.
